nightfever

2019 | a fictitious nightclub combining botanical gardens and metal

concept

Metal is a genre that accepts anyone and anything. It is not confined to one way of thinking, and helps us see the world from different perspectives. Botanical gardens tend to represent nature from all over the world. How do we combine the two?

idea

Inspired by Slavic folklore and symbols, this brand goes back to our spiritual roots. The all seeying eye stands as a symbol for wisdom and enlightment combined with the monstrous figures. The logo is strechtable and can fit and accept any space it has to fill. This combination represents our concept of metal and botanical gardens.
