Nightfever

Nightfever is an fictive club that at its core combines metal music with a botanical garden. This is the first time I learned to combine multiple elements together to create a brand. The main idea comes from Slavic culture. Combining nature, symbols like the all-seeing eye and monsters. The brand itself centers around the logo that is stretchable in any way. This translates into some goodies where our logo is always visible, but still fun and different to look at.
