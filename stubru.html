Subru Olympic Snowcase

Studio Brussels, a Belgian radio station, is organizing it's next edition of their Snowcase. A week of partying and drinking up in the mountains. What could we do this year to make it different? An Olympic Snowcase would do just fine at that. Combining the main elements of drinking and partying with some fun bar games. We're working with the already existing branding of StuBru, only adding some olympic elements under the slogan "participating is far more important than losing". We have posters that, when combined, create a big spanner. Our olympic torch is a vinyl plate that will be brought to the mountains by several runners and will play there constantly for the coming week. Combining this with a Vedett sponsorship and creating a fivepack, reffering to the five olympic rings. Drunken fun assured! This project was done in collaboration with Arno.
