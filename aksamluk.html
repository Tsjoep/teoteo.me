Akšamluk

How would you visually describe yourself in a poster whilst leaving a message for others? Combining my two cultural backgrounds and combining them, I wanted to visualise this poster with the concept op drinking alcohol. Since alcohol is both in Belgium and Bosnia a way of socializing with eachother. Now to bring these two worlds together I found an old Bosnian word, "akšamluk". Akšamluk is the act of coming together with friends and drinking something near the water in the evening. This is then visaualized in a poster where two colours of bottles and glasses come together to in a third one, just like friends come together to akšamluk.
