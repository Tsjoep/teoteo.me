Akšamluk

2020 | Describe yourself in a poster and leave a message for others

Concept

By combining my two cultural backgrounds I would like to leave a message of tolerance, love and friendship. What better way to do that than with alcohol?

Idea

Akšamluk is the act of coming together at nightfall to drink and to enjoy the company of others. Belgium is represented by beerbottles and -glasses whilst Bosnia is represented by rakia cans and shotglasses. These two worlds, represented by two colours, come together in a third one. By combining the two we reach our akšamluk.
