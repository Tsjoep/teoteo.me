#deliverules

2019 | get out and create a guerilla campaign | in collaboration with Arno

concept

Is there any way we could advertise a brand by using other brands?

idea

A social media campaign where you have to go on a hunt for lazy non-Deliveroo drivers to get a discount. This increases public relations and is free advertising for Deliveroo.
