#DELIVERULES

Get out and find a way to guerilla advertise any brand. Walking through the city you seea lot of bike couriers just sitting around, that's where the idea of #DELIVERULES came to life. You take a picture of a lazy bike courier working for Takeaway or UBER Eats and sent it to Deliveroo to receive a 5% discount on your next order. This project was done in collaboration with Arno.
