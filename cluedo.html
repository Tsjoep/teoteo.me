boardgame cluedo

2019 | make your very own cluedo boardgame | in collaboration with Charlotte, Fran and Nina

concept

The problem is not so much creating the game, but being able to work together using one defined design language.

idea

Combining vector only drawings in a grid, limited to a specific colourway, creates a strong design language that can be distributed and learned by anyone. Finished off with a luxury velvet feel.
