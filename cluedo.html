Boardgame Cluedo

This time around, I redesigned a game of cluedo with three other students. We had the rules and the overal idea, but nothing visual. It was hassle to learn to work together and learn eachothers strengths and weaknesses. But in the end, we made something to be proud of. Our game plays in a chique hotel where a bunch of rich people and hotel employees, all with their own motives, have come together and are playing poker with high stakes. The gamemaster dies that night. Who killed him, where did they kill him and with what item?
