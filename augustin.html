O Du Lieber Augustin

2020 | Create something for the unlockdown of Vienna | lieberaugustin.at

Concept

The big unlockdown is not only to be celebrated, we should also thank those that made it happen.

Idea

Through the legend of Augustin, the man that lived through the Plague, we bring ode to the heroes of the corona period, our Augustins. This ode will be brought in the most Bavarian way possible, collective singing. Karaoke included :)
